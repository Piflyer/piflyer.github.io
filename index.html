Projects

SmartDisplayPi

Turn your Raspberry Pi to a Smart Display with visual cards for weather, traffic, and your calendar. Plus, Home Assistant and Google Assistant are built in, making this the ultimate smart home device.

Project Intraweb

Project Intraweb provides software for schools, businesses, and communities to provide offline content, or a working Intraweb. All of this is powered by a Raspberry Pi.

Project Seaward

Project Seaward aims to visualize the effects of climate change through storytelling and Augmented Reality (AR). Project Seaward also helps inform and find solutions towards our climate change crisis.

Know Your Rights

Know Your Rights aims to inform people about their rights in certain situations. Available as a webiste, chabot, and a Google Assistant skill, you can access Know Your Rights anytime and anywhere. You can even share your experinces through the website and also view it offline though the website.
